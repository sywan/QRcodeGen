# Chrome Web Store Listing Draft

## Description

QR Studio is a polished QR code generator for Chrome that helps you create clean, branded QR codes without uploading your content to an online service.

Use it to turn a URL into a downloadable QR code, then customize the result with color themes, QR module styles, a center image or logo, and a short personal inscription. QR Studio is designed for everyday publishing work: event flyers, classroom materials, business cards, product labels, menus, presentations, portfolio links, and internal team resources.

Version 1.1.2 adds Chrome extension update notifications. When Chrome has a newer QR Studio extension build ready, the extension popup can notify the user and offer a simple choice to ignore the notice or upgrade by reloading the extension into the updated build.

Why install QR Studio?

- Create QR codes quickly from typed, pasted, or dragged URLs.
- Add a center image or logo, scale it up, crop it naturally, and drag it into position.
- Adjust the safe center area so the icon and inscription stay within a scan-friendly region.
- Add a short personal inscription, move it within the center area, tune its font size, and choose from preset or custom text colors.
- Choose from multiple visual styles, including square, rounded, dotted, soft, mosaic, and capsule QR modules.
- Choose from multiple color themes for a more polished look.
- Export or copy the finished QR code as a PNG.
- Use a compact, focused interface that opens directly from Chrome.
- Work locally in your browser. QR Studio does not require an account, backend service, or cloud upload.

QR Studio is useful for anyone who wants QR codes that look more intentional than a plain black-and-white utility output, while still keeping the workflow simple. The app keeps the current square QR style as the default, then gives you careful controls for visual customization when you need them.

Privacy note: QR Studio runs locally as a Chrome extension and does not collect, transmit, sell, or share your URL, images, inscriptions, or generated QR codes.
